2-3 weeks after placing the order. This isn't Amazon, be patient.

Your package is not actually in the US yet, it may take 10 or more days for the tracking to update. "Arrived at USPS facility" means it made it through customs.

The vendor will reprint and reship your order, no need to worry. Reach out to them here or over email with a screenshot of the tracking so they can better assist you.

What does the 'duplicate' option mean at checkout?

What is a "Lense"?

A Lense on an ID is a security feature that some states use. It's placed at the bottom right of an ID.

All vendors use stealth shipping methods. If the order can get past customs, it can get past your nosy parents.

Use the front facing camera on your phone and stand 4 feet away from a solid colored background. Make sure there are no open windows and there are no shadows on your face. The most important part of the photo is even lighting on your face. Use the flash.

My tracking has not updated for a while, should I be worried?

No, this is normal as it has to pass through customs.
